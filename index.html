Technology

Our goal is to optimize traffic flow in large cities by developing an urban traffic brain system that provides traffic control intelligence through large-scale traffic simulation and cloud-edge based real-time traffic analysis

Main Goal

  • Average travel time reduction rate: over 15%
  • Number of optimized signal intersections: over 200
  • Support cloud-edge based distributed processing of multi-agent signal optimization
  • Number of signal optimization visual analysis modules: over 2
  • Congestion propagation prediction accuracy: over 0.85 (F1-score )
  • Traffic demand estimation error: under 10% (MAPE)

Research Area

  • Development of traffic network signal optimization technology and scalable simulation technology to verify the ripple effect of the entire city through edge-cloud collaboration
  • Development of traffic situation recognition and prediction technology through edge-cloud and edge-edge collaborations
  • Cloud-based data collection, conversion, and management using various public and private traffic data
  • Visual tool support for edge-to-cloud collaborative traffic monitoring and city-wide ripple effect analysis of signal optimization
  • Cloud-edge virtualization technology that enables dynamic expansion of urban traffic brain
  • The results of the development of the urban traffic brain are applied to local government signal control and traffic infrastructure to practice

Traffic Signal Optimization

We optimize traffic signal using the reinforcement learning technology.

  • Signal optimization based on deep reinforcement learning that quickly adapts to changes in traffic volume
  • Applicable to actual intersections by satisfying signal constraints such as pedestrian signals and signal cycles
  • Deep learning considering the constraints of signals can be applied to actual signal systems
  • Cloud-based distributed processing supports optimization of more than 200 large-scale intersections

Large-scale Traffic Simulation

Large-scale traffic simulation capable of analyzing the traffic flow of the entire metropolitan area

  • Large-scale traffic simulation that can be applied to the entire large city (applied to Daejeon/Sejong City)
  • Multiscale simulation model support that satisfies both accuracy and processing performance
  • Accurate traffic demand estimation close to real traffic patterns (MAPE 10% or less)
  • Dynamic interface support for real-time control even while simulation is running

Traffic Analysis

Fast and accurate cloud edge-based traffic analysis

  • Traffic analysis in edge servers near intersections in connection with smart intersection systems in local governments
  • Deep learning-based traffic situation recognition for various moving objects including vehicles
  • Accurate traffic analysis through congestion propagation prediction based on deep learning
  • Supports intuitive visual analysis tools for decision making by signal operators
